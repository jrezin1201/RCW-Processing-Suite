Available Processing Tools

⚡
Gas & Rig / Hours Worked

Processes time and materials job data from Excel exports. Automatically extracts 4-digit job numbers from location fields, aggregates total hours per job, and calculates billing at $0.75 per hour. Outputs a formatted Excel summary with properly sorted job numbers and currency-formatted totals. Supports both standard and RC Wendt data formats.

🏗️
Contracts-Forms Processing

Transforms construction project task lists into formatted billing summaries. Automatically extracts project name, phase, and house string from input files. Categorizes tasks into EXT PRIME, EXTERIOR, EXTERIOR UA, and INTERIOR buckets. Formats lot numbers, combines plan/elevation codes, and generates professional Excel outputs with project metadata, totals by lot/plan, and automatic labor (43%) and material (28%) calculations. Includes comprehensive QA reporting for unmapped tasks. Supports both .XLS and .XLSX formats.

📝
Missed Clock-In Warning Notices

Parses timekeeping Exception List exports and generates formatted employee warning notices for Missing Start Touch and Missing Stop Touch violations. Produces a single Excel workbook with an Overview sheet (breakdown by error type, repeat offenders, violations by date) plus one fully-formatted 34-row warning notice per violation, auto-populated with employee name, date, clock times, and bilingual English/Spanish policy descriptions. Clocked In Twice records are tracked in the Overview for reference.
